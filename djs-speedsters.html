About DJS Speedsters

DJS Speedsters is the official E-Superbike team of D. J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, founded in 2017 by four passionate bike enthusiasts with a shared vision to build something revolutionary. What began as a small initiative has now evolved into a powerhouse of over 40+ dedicated engineering students who design and fabricate electric superbikes entirely from scratch.

In 2020, the team achieved a historic milestone by building India’s first collegiate electric superbike. Competing annually at FMAE MotoStudent India, held at Kari Motor Speedway and officially powered by KTM, the team embraces the spirit of real-world motorsports. From chassis and aerodynamics to electronics and drivetrain, every component is self-designed and manufactured by students. This hands-on approach has earned them national podiums and deep-rooted technical expertise. At DJS Speedsters, they don’t just build bikes—they accelerate electrically and ride with soul.
