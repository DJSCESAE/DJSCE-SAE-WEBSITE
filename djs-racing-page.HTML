About DJS Racing

DJS Racing is the official formula student team of D. J. Sanghvi College of Engineering. We design, build, and refine electric formula-style race cars from the ground up, combining technical excellence with bold engineering vision.

Each year, we proudly represent India at premier competitions like Formula Bharat, Formula Student Germany, and FS Austria. From vehicle dynamics and aerodynamics to powertrain and systems integration, we engineer every component with precision, passion, and purpose — carrying the spirit of Indian innovation into the world of Formula Student motorsport.

Our Departments

Structural

Deals with the design, analysis, and manufacturing of the vehicle's framework, composites, and aerodynamic parts to ensure driver safety and maximize speed.

Brakes

Ensures the braking system and pedal assembly are safe, ergonomic, and capable of decelerating the vehicle reliably at its maximum possible rate.

Suspension & Steering

Controls the vehicle’s handling for safety and performance while ensuring driver comfort. The steering system allows for easy and precise maneuvering of the car.

Drivetrain

Responsible for transmitting power from our AC synchronous motor to the wheels through custom-designed components for a light, reliable, and smooth transmission.

Mechanical Integration

Designs and manufactures the accumulator container, integrates electronic components, and manages the thermal systems for the accumulator and motor.

Electronics

Manages the High Voltage (battery pack, BMS) and Low Voltage (safety circuits, ECU, DAQ) systems, all connected by a self-designed wire harness.

Driverless

Develops the autonomous vehicle systems, focusing on Perception, Estimation & Planning, and Controls to enable the car to drive itself around the track.

Marketing

Handles all non-technical tasks including social media, public relations, and obtaining sponsorships to fund the project, developing creative and sales skills.
