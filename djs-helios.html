About DJS Helios

DJS Helios India is the official solar electric vehicle team of Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, Mumbai. Founded in 2016 as a final-year engineering project by four passionate B.E. students. With a strong team of 20+ dedicated members, we are proud to be Mumbai’s first student-run team committed to the design, development, and fabrication of high-performance solar-powered electric vehicles.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of green automotive technology while providing students with hands-on experience in engineering, innovation, and teamwork. Over the years, we have participated in numerous national and international competitions like (ESVC 3000, BSVC, IASC). At DJS Helios, we continue to strive for innovation, aiming to shape the future of solar electric mobility while upholding the values of teamwork, technical excellence, and environmental responsibility.
