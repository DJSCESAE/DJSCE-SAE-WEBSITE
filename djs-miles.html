About DJS Miles

DJS Miles is the official super-mileage vehicle team of Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, competing in Shell Eco Marathon. We conceptualize, design and manufacture electric vehicles with the ultimate goal of achieving the highest possible mileage.

Additionally, we conduct drives and campaigns aimed at promoting a sustainable future. We are the first team to entirely manufacture a chassis using carbon-fibre.
