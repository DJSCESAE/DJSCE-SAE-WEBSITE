About DJS Astra

DJS Astra is the official combat robotics team of DJ Sanghvi. Robowars is a high-intensity, competitive event where custom-built combat robots engage in one-on-one battles in a controlled arena. Combining mechanical engineering, electronics, and strategic design, Robowars pushes the boundaries of robotics innovation and teamwork.

Participants must design, fabricate, and operate autonomous or remote-controlled bots capable of offensive and defensive maneuvers, often within strict weight and weapon constraints. But that’s not what we have limited ourselves to. In 2025, Astra have expanded into the world on Robosoccer which combines mechanical craftsmanship with the beautiful game. Our goal is to Push for more competitions every year and give ourselves a tougher challenge so that we can soon compete on the grand stage at the Techfest, IIT Bombay.
