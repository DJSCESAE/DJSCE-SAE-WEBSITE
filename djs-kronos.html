About DJS Kronos

DJS Kronos India is the official BAJA SAE off-road racing team of Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering. We are a dynamic team of 40 passionate engineers who design, manufacture, validate, and rigorously test an electric and autonomous all-terrain vehicle every year.

Competing in prestigious events like e-BAJA, a-BAJA, and ATVC, we proudly represent our college on both national and international platforms. With every season, we strive to learn, innovate, and excel to bring home new achievements, building a successful legacy we are truly proud of.

Our Departments

Chassis

Designs and fabricates the structural frame of the vehicle, ensuring strength, safety, and durability for extreme off-road conditions.

Suspension

Develops the suspension system to provide optimal handling, stability, and traction over rough and unpredictable terrain.

Steering

Engineers a responsive and reliable steering system that allows for precise control and maneuverability in challenging off-road environments.

Brakes

Focuses on designing a powerful and consistent braking system that ensures the safety and stopping power required for high-performance off-roading.

Transmission

Responsible for the gearbox and drivetrain components, optimizing power delivery from the engine to the wheels for maximum performance.

Electronics

Manages all electrical systems, including the vehicle's wiring, data acquisition, and control units for both electric and autonomous functionalities.
