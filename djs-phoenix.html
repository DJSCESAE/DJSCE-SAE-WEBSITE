About DJS Phoenix

DJS Phoenix is the official drone team of Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering. We are a lively and passionate team of 51 innovators and dreamers united by our love for drones and aviation technology. At DJS Phoenix, we design, build, and rigorously test autonomous aerial systems, pushing the boundaries of UAV innovation every season.

Competing in esteemed national and international competitions such as COEX CopterHack, SAE Aerothon, AeroGCS, NIDAR, and the ISRO Robotics Challenge, we proudly represent our college on global platforms. With each passing year, we strive to dream bigger, innovate smarter, and set new benchmarks, building a legacy of excellence that inspires future engineers.
