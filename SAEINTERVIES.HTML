About the Recruitment Drive

SAE organizes a large-scale recruitment event, "Interview 2025," aimed at inducting enthusiastic and capable First-Year students into our 11 diverse technical teams. The event is a cornerstone of our commitment to identifying fresh talent and introducing them to the world of technical innovation, teamwork, and practical engineering experience.

Over three days, more than 300 students are interviewed across all classrooms on the 4th floor of the Mechanical Engineering department. This structured process allows our teams to assess not just the technical potential of applicants, but also their problem-solving skills and ability to collaborate.

Dates

2nd to 4th March 2025

Venue

4th Floor, Mechanical Dept.

Who Can Apply?

Primarily aimed at First-Year students from all departments.

Interview Guidelines

Interview Rules

  • Applicants must upload a resume and final team preference order in the form.
  • The resume must contain the candidate's name and photo, but NO contact information.
  • Strictly dress in complete formal attire, including formal shoes.
  • You are required to give interviews for your first 4 preferences.
  • Bring at least 3 copies of your resume for each interview (minimum 12 total).
  • If selected for your 1st preference, you must accept the position.
  • You can only be part of one SAE or IETE team.

Blacklisting Conditions

  • Failure to attend interviews for all top four preferences.
  • Attempting to contact any team before or after the interviews.
  • Attempting to switch teams after selection.

How to Build a Resume

  • Keep it to a single page. Use a simple, effective design from Canva or MS Word.
  • Add your name in a large, bold font and include a clear, recent photo.
  • Mention your education, skills, and familiarity with technical/creative software.
  • Highlight personality traits, interests, projects, and any teamwork or volunteer experience.
  • Be prepared to support everything on your resume. Do not lie or add irrelevant information.
