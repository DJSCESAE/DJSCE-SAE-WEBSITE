About DJS Skylark

DJS Skylark, our college's official aero design team, operates like a real-world aerospace engineering firm — taking on real challenges, real constraints, and delivering real innovation. We design, build, and perfect radio-controlled aircraft from scratch, blending technical precision with creative flair.

We proudly represent India at the prestigious SAE Aero Design international collegiate competition held annually in the USA — where our ideas take flight on a global stage.
