About DJS Karting

DJS Karting India, our college’s official go-kart team, operates like a real-world motorsport engineering unit, taking on technical challenges, adhering to strict regulations, and driving innovation at every stage. With a strong team of 30+ dedicated members, we design, fabricate, and race high-performance go-karts, combining engineering excellence with competitive ambition.

Majorly all components are meticulously built in-house, reflecting our commitment to performance, safety, and creativity. We proudly represent our institution at prestigious competitions like the Go Kart Design Challenge (GKDC), where we strive to secure podium finishes and uphold our legacy at the national level.
