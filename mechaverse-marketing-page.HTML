About The Event

MECHAVERSE is a flagship technical fest organized by the Department of Mechanical Engineering, open to students from all academic programs. It is a celebration of innovation, creativity, and everything related to the field of Mechanical Engineering.

After two successful editions, MECHAVERSE returns this year bigger and better than ever. Get ready for an exciting lineup of new events, along with the return of popular crowd favorites like Hi-Design, Automobile Quiz, the F1 Simulator, and the thrilling Murder Mystery.

Why Partner With Us?

Partnering with SAE DJSCE means associating your brand with one of Mumbai's most reputed engineering colleges. Our SAE chapter represents a diverse pool of highly driven and technically skilled students who consistently excel in national and international competitions.

Your support not only fuels innovation but also aligns your brand with quality, performance, and excellence, providing high brand visibility and access to an elite talent pool of future industry leaders.
